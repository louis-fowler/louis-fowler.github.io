Emotional Intelligence

23/4/20

What is Emotional Intelligence?

"The ability to monitor one’s own and other’s feelings and emotions, to discriminate among them and to use this information to guide one’s thinking and actions” Salovey and Mayer 1990

Basically as the quote above explains, Emotional Intelligence (EI) is the understanding of ones self emotions and feeling as well as others. This makes it possible to guide how one acts, behaves and thinks in different situations that best suits

How is it different to IQ?

Whilst IQ is purely about how smart one is based on their Intelligence, EI is how much one understands emotions and feelings, their intelligence in regards to human emotions.

Why is it important?

When working in a group environment it is important to have a good understanding of how you are feeling as well as those around you. If you are able to adapt your emotions to best suit the circumstances it will be much easier to achieve ones goal.
